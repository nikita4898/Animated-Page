While the hard drive may seem like a cost-effective way to store your data, there are constant challenges around how your performance and capacity evolve. The Hard Drive tends to operate at a constant rate - even though it gets larger and larger on an annual basis.

For customers who are deploying an exclusively hard drive based infrastructure, their performance per bit in which they deploy on an annual basis just goes down - and that gap between performance and capacity just grows and grows...something needs to be done...something has been done...enter flash.
